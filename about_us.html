Our Journey

How we got here

From a two-person freelance duo to a full-scale agency — here's how BuildScale came to be.

2018 — The Beginning

Two friends, one mission

Founders Arjun Mehta and Priya Sharma started BuildScale from a co-working space in Mumbai with a simple belief: small businesses deserved the same quality websites as big corporations. Their first client was a local restaurant. It's still with us today.

2020 — Pivot & Growth

Going all-in on SaaS & startups

During the pandemic boom in digital transformation, BuildScale pivoted to serve tech startups aggressively. We helped 40+ startups launch their first digital product, and our team grew from 3 to 12 people — fully remote-first.

2022 — Enterprise Scale

Winning our first enterprise clients

Landing our first Fortune 500 engagement validated that our process worked at any scale. We restructured the agency into dedicated squads for SMB, startup, and enterprise segments — letting each team go deep in their domain.

2024 — Today

A full-service digital growth partner

Now 24 strong, BuildScale has delivered over 400 projects across 18 countries. We're not just web builders — we're growth partners who stay in the game long after launch day. The best is still ahead.
