FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Filter by topic

How quickly can you start my project after I sign up? +
We onboard new clients within 3–5 business days of contract signing and initial payment. For urgent projects, a fast-track option can begin within 48 hours — just mention it in your brief.
What is the typical delivery time for a project? +
Starter websites take 7–14 days. Growth projects run 3–4 weeks. Enterprise builds range from 6 weeks to several months. Your contract will include a specific delivery date with milestone breakpoints.
How do I request a revision or change? +
Submit your revision requests through the ticket form above or email your dedicated project manager directly. Be as specific as possible — screenshots and annotated images are always helpful. Minor revisions are typically turned around within 24–48 hours.
What is your payment structure? +
Standard projects are billed 50% upfront at contract signing and 50% upon project completion before the final handover. Enterprise retainer engagements are billed monthly in advance.
Do you offer refunds? +
The upfront deposit is non-refundable once work has commenced, as it covers discovery, planning, and initial design work. However, if we fail to meet agreed milestones, we'll work to make it right — including partial refunds where appropriate. Contact support to discuss your situation.
Will I be able to edit my website after launch? +
Yes. All our sites include a CMS (Content Management System) so you can update text, images, blog posts, and other content without touching code. We'll provide a training walkthrough before handover.
What happens if my site goes down after launch? +
If your site goes down during the support period included in your plan, submit an urgent ticket and we'll respond within 2 hours. After the support period ends, we offer maintenance retainers to keep everything running smoothly.
Can I see work-in-progress before the final delivery? +
Absolutely. We share design mockups for approval before development begins, and a staging link is provided once development is underway so you can review the live build at any time.
Do you work with my existing hosting provider? +
In most cases, yes. We work with Hostinger, GoDaddy, Bluehost, cPanel, Vercel, Netlify, AWS, and most other major providers. If you're on an unsupported platform, we'll let you know and recommend alternatives.
What if I need more revisions than my plan includes? +
Additional revisions beyond your plan's included rounds are billed at an hourly rate (disclosed in your contract). We'll always get your approval before commencing paid revision work — no surprise invoices.
