04 — Revisions

Revisions & Change Requests

Each plan includes a defined number of revision rounds as specified in your proposal. A "revision round" means one consolidated set of feedback submitted at one time. Multiple rounds of feedback submitted separately each count as separate revision rounds.

What Counts as a Revision

  • Minor revision: Text changes, colour adjustments, image swaps, layout tweaks within the agreed design.
  • Major revision: Structural redesigns, new pages, feature additions, change of direction, or anything requiring significant new design or development work.

Major revisions beyond the included rounds are billed at our standard hourly rate (disclosed in your contract). We will always provide a written quote and seek your approval before commencing additional work.

Post-Launch Changes

Changes requested after the project has been delivered and signed off are treated as new work and quoted accordingly, unless covered by an active maintenance retainer.

01 — Overview

Refund Policy

We stand behind the quality of our work. This policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds are available and how to request them. Please read this carefully before engaging our services.

Our Approach We aim to resolve any dissatisfaction before considering a refund. If you're not happy with any aspect of our work, please contact your project manager first — we'll do everything reasonable to make it right.
02 — Deposit Policy

Initial Deposit

The initial deposit (typically 50% of the project fee) is non-refundable once project work has commenced. This deposit covers:

  • Discovery sessions and requirements gathering
  • Resource allocation and project scheduling
  • Initial design concepts and wireframes
  • Strategic planning and competitor research

If you cancel before any work has commenced (within 48 hours of payment and before the project kickoff call), we will refund the deposit in full, less any payment processing fees.

03 — Mid-Project

Cancellation During a Project

If you cancel a project after work has commenced:

Stage at Cancellation Amount Refundable
Before design phase begins Up to 80% of remaining balance
During design phase Up to 50% of remaining balance
During development phase Up to 25% of remaining balance
After QA / near completion No refund — full balance due

All completed work-in-progress will be handed over to you upon settlement of the final invoice for work completed to date.

04 — Quality Issues

Quality & Dispute Refunds

If the delivered work materially fails to meet the agreed specifications and we are unable to remedy the issue within a reasonable timeframe, you may be entitled to a partial or full refund. To be eligible:

  • The issue must be raised within the warranty period (30–90 days, depending on your plan)
  • The defect must be demonstrably our error, not caused by client modification or third-party changes
  • You must have allowed us a reasonable opportunity (at least 14 days) to fix the issue first

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email billing@buildscale.io with the subject line "Refund Request — [Your Name / Project]"
  2. Include a clear description of the issue and what resolution you're seeking
  3. Our team will respond within 5 business days with a decision or request for more information
  4. Approved refunds are processed within 10–14 business days via the original payment method
Non-Refundable Situations Refunds are not available for: change of mind after work has commenced, dissatisfaction with results due to client-provided content or direction, delays caused by the client, or third-party platform issues beyond our control.
